# Fighter

The quintessential warrior, masters of arms and armor.

## Class Features

```rpg class.features
class: Fighter
features:
  - level: 1
    $name: Fighting Style
    $contents: Choose a fighting style that reflects your combat training.
  - level: 1
    $name: Second Wind
    $contents: Regain 1d10 + your fighter level hit points as a bonus action. Usable once per short rest.
  - level: 2
    $name: Action Surge
    $contents: Take one additional action on your turn. Usable once per short rest (twice at level 17).
  - level: 3
    $name: Martial Archetype
    $contents: Choose an archetype that reflects your approach to combat.
  - level: 4
    $name: Ability Score Improvement
    $contents: Increase one ability score by 2, or two ability scores by 1 each.
  - level: 5
    $name: Extra Attack
    $contents: You can attack twice, instead of once, whenever you take the Attack action.
  - level: 6
    $name: Ability Score Improvement
    $contents: Increase one ability score by 2, or two ability scores by 1 each.
  - level: 7
    $name: Martial Archetype Feature
    $contents: Your chosen archetype grants you an additional feature.
```
